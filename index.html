About CEAA

The Centre of Excellence in Astronomy and Astrophysics (CEAA) at Christ University stands as a premier hub for cutting-edge research and education in space sciences. Established under the Department of Physics and Electronics, the centre is dedicated to fostering a rigorous academic environment where students and faculty collaborate on breakthrough projects in observational and theoretical astrophysics. Through strategic global partnerships and advanced training programs, CEAA nurtures the intellectual curiosity of the next generation of scientists, aiming to make significant contributions to our understanding of the universe.
